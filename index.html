About Me

Math tutoring with patience, structure, and real understanding

I help middle school and high school students become more confident problem-solvers — not just students who memorize steps for one test and forget them later.

Hi, I’m Sonny Jung, a Mathematics and Scientific Computation student at UC Davis. My own math journey taught me that success in math is not about being “naturally good” at every topic right away. It comes from learning how to slow down, find the exact point of confusion, and rebuild the concept until it finally makes sense.

That is the same approach I bring into tutoring. Whether a student is struggling with fractions, algebra, geometry, precalculus, or SAT/ACT math, I focus on making each step clear and connected. I want students to understand why a method works, not just copy a formula.

The goal of each session is simple: identify what is confusing, practice it in a calm and organized way, and leave with a clear plan for what to do next. Over time, students should feel more prepared for homework, quizzes, exams, and bigger goals like standardized tests.
